Visualforce on Chromecast

Cast any Visualforce page to a Google Chromecast.

  1. Buy a Google Chromecast and set it up on your wifi network.
  2. Ensure that you're running Chrome 28 or higher.
  3. Install the Google Cast Extension
  4. Connect your laptop to the same wifi network as your Google Chromecast

Click here, log in to a Salesforce org, and select a Visualforce Page to Cast.

Notes

  • You will need the 'API Enabled' permission to use Visualforce on Chromecast.
  • If you choose a page with a Standard Controller, you'll be able to select its record.
